TASTE FOR LIFE

My favorite restaurant is called taste for life .
It is a bright and fun place where they make the best chicken biryani in town.
I love how it smells when I walk in! You can see them toss the chicken in the air and add all the tasty toppings. They even let kids make their own mini kababs.
The people who work there are always friendly and make you feel special. The best part is when the hot, yummy biryani comes to the table.Every bite is full of goodness. Going to taste for life always makes me happy.
